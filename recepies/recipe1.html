Spaghetti Carbonara

Description

Spaghetti Carbonara is a classic Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and pepper. It's creamy, savory, and incredibly satisfying.

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. In a pan, cook the pancetta until crispy.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs and Parmesan cheese.
  4. Drain the spaghetti and add it to the pan with pancetta.
  5. Remove from heat and quickly mix in the egg mixture, stirring until creamy.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Serve immediately with extra Parmesan cheese on top.
