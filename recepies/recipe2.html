Chicken Tikka Masala

Description

Chicken Tikka Masala is a popular Indian dish made with marinated chicken cooked in a spiced tomato sauce. It's rich, flavorful, and pairs perfectly with rice or naan bread.

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken breast, cubed
  • 200g yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons tikka masala paste
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 400g canned tomatoes
  • 200ml coconut milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Marinate the chicken in yogurt and tikka masala paste for at least 1 hour.
  2. In a pan, cook the onion and garlic until soft.
  3. Add the marinated chicken and cook until browned.
  4. Add the canned tomatoes and coconut milk, simmer for 20 minutes.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Serve with rice or naan bread.
