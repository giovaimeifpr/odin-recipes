Vegetable Stir Fry

Description

Vegetable Stir Fry is a quick and healthy dish that combines a variety of fresh vegetables sautéed in a savory sauce. It's perfect for a light meal and can be served with rice or noodles.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat sesame oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  2. Add garlic and sauté until fragrant.
  3. Add broccoli, bell pepper, carrot, and snap peas.
  4. Stir fry the vegetables for about 5-7 minutes until tender-crisp.
  5. Pour in the soy sauce and stir to coat the vegetables.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Serve hot with rice or noodles.
